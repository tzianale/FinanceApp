About Finance Fun App

This app is a simple way to track stock market stuff in real-time. Using a free API, it lets you see the latest on stock prices and market trends without making things too complicated.

Whether you're casually following the market or just curious about how stocks are doing, this app provides a straightforward view. It's built for fun and learning, not for hardcore trading.
