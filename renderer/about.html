About Finance App

This app is a simple way to track stock prices in real-time. Using a free Finnhub API, it lets you see the latest on stock prices and market trends.

Stock price Stuck on 'loading'? It might be because the market's closed or the stock symbol isn't right or supported by the free API. Double-check to make sure it's correct and supported!

If you're curious about the tech stack used for this app or interested in checking out other projects, head over to www.tzialexis.ch.

This application is for fun and educational purposes, not intended for professional trading. Always do your own research and consult a financial advisor before making any investment decisions.
